## Common types of lead magnets?

* Membership / App
* Training / Certification
* Document / E-book / Case Study
* Free trial
* Discount
* Survey

## What are the traits of a good lead magnet?

* Free
* Exclusive / High Value
* Functional
* Immediate
* Evergreen / "Bookmarkable"
* Trackable
